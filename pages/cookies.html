Chocolate Chip Cookies

Description

Wow, look at those chocolate chips. Right in those cookies. Chocolate chip cookies. Wow. You could eat those. You will eat those. Stay tuned.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup butter, softened
  • 1 cup white sugar
  • 1 cup packed brown sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 2 teaspoons hot water
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 3 cups all purpose flour
  • 2 cups semisweet chocolate chips
  • 1 cup chopped walnuts
  • A burning passion for the culinary arts

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C)
  2. Cream together the butter, white sugar, and brown sugar until smooth. Beat in the eggs over time, then stir in the vanilla. Dissolve baking soda in hot water. Add to batter along with salt. Stir in flour, chocolate chips, and nuts. Drop by large spoonfuls onto ungreased pans. I have no idea why this is all in step 2.
  3. Bake for about 10 minutes in the preheated oven, or until edges are nicely browned.
  4. ENJOY. NOW. OR WAIT JUST A BIT. PLEASE DON'T BURN YOURSELF. I CARE ABOUT YOU.
