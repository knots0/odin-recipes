Easy Coleslaw Dressing

Description

Easy coleslaw dressing. So easy. Could never be hard. It doesn't get any easier than this. Now you can finally enjoy your salad without having to worry about the pregame intricacies. Easy.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup mayonnaise
  • 2 tablespoons white sugar
  • 1/2 tablespoons lemon juice
  • 1 tablespoon vinegar
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt

Steps

  1. Whisk mayonnaise, sugar, lemon juice, vinegar, pepper, and salt together in a bowl until smooth and creamy
