Cereal

Description

This will be your finished product. It looks very complicated, but I promise you'll be able to get the hang of it with some practice!

Ingredients

  • Milk
  • Cereal Of Your Choice
  • Bowl (optional)
  • Spoon (also optional)

Steps

  1. Get bowl
  2. Pour cereal in bowl
  3. Pour milk in bowl
  4. Do not reverse steps 2 and 3
  5. Use spoon on cereal (gently)
